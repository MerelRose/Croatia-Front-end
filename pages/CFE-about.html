About Us

Welcome to SiftSearch, a platform where product discovery, community engagement, and ethical buying come together. Created by a passionate group of Croatian students and Erasmus exchange students from the Netherlands, SiftSearch was born from our shared love for smart shopping and collaboration across borders.

Our Mission

At SiftSearch, we aim to empower shoppers by providing a transparent, user-driven platform for rating, commenting, and selling products. We believe that the best recommendations come from real people with real experiences, and our goal is to create a trustworthy environment where users can share their insights, give honest feedback, and find the best products based on collective wisdom.

What We Offer

Product Ratings: Browse thousands of products, rated by our community. Whether you’re searching for tech gadgets, fashion, or household goods, you’ll find real reviews to guide your purchases.

Comment and Engage: Have a product you love (or don’t)? Add your voice to the conversation! SiftSearch allows users to comment on products, fostering discussion and helping others make informed decisions.

Buy and Sell: SiftSearch isn’t just a review site—it’s a marketplace too. Find products you want to purchase, or sell your own items to a like-minded community.

Built by Students, for Shoppers

Our international team of Croatian and Dutch students brings together different perspectives, united by a commitment to enhancing the online shopping experience. We’re not just building a platform—we’re building a community where transparency, reliability, and mutual support are at the core.

Join us, sift through the best products, share your thoughts, and become part of the SiftSearch movement!
